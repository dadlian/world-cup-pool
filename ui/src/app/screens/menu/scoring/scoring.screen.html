Group Stage

The World Cup 2022 group stage runs from Sunday 20th November to Friday 2nd December. Teams are divided into 8 groups and there are a total of 48 matches. For each of these matches you must select the team you think will win (or select no winner if you predict a draw) and your projected score. Group stage scoring is as follows:

Scoring

  • Correctly predict match winner (or a draw): 1 point
  • Correctly predict match score: 1 point
  • Correctly predict team's group finish: 2 points
  • Total points available: 160

Knockouts

The World Cup 2022 knockout stage runs from Saturday 3rd December to Sunday 18th December. The top two teams from each group are seeded into a single-elimination knockout bracket that starts with the Round of 16 and culminates in the World Cup Final. Similar to the group stage, you will predict a winner and a score for each of the 16 knockout games, which will determine which teams you think will advance in the tournament. Knockout scoring is as follows:

Scoring

  • Correctly predict match score (after a.e.t): 1 point
  • Each correctly predicted quater-finalist: 2 points
  • Each correctly predicted semi-finalist: 4 points
  • Each correctly predicted finalist: 8 points
  • BONUS Correctly predict third place: 4 points
  • BONUS Correctly predict world cup winner: 12 points
  • Total points available: 80

Tiebreaker

In the unlikely event of a tie, a tie-breaker prediction will be used to determine the winner. The tie-breaker prediction is the total attendance of all 64 world cup matches. For reference Russia 2018 had a total attendance figure of 3,031,768 (47,371 per match).
